How to Bless Someone

Want to know how to bless someone for the Lord, in the Lord? Then you are fortunate to be here. This is how:

  1. Find someone you would like to bless. It doesn't really matter who they are because you are going to do something nice for them and help them out a lot.

  2. Know who the Lord is because it is mighty hard to bless without first being blessed from God.

  3. Say a prayer for the person you would like to bless. Something such as, "Father bless this person in their life and give them good things and a good life in Jesus Christ's name, Amen."

  4. You don't have to tell that person that you blessed them. If someone asked you to bless them then you can.

  5. Another way to bless is be a blessing yourself read more here http://www.wikihow.com/Be-a-Blessing-to-Your-Friends.

Blessings on you for reading :)
