Yale Projects Board

The Yale Projects Board is a platform to connect developers with entrepeneurs and academics at Yale. We hope to unlock some of the peer-to-peer potential present on a college campus of thousands of brilliant students and help Yalies build some awesome things.

Built with: Ruby on Rails, Angular JS, SASS

View the project: yale.projectsboard.io (login required)

3Derm

3Derm is a Boston-based startup dedicated to improving the detection skin cancer in its earliest stages. The company uses a custom-built 3D imaging device for easy and affordable screening. I helped design and build the front-end of an online portal for patients and doctors.

Built with: MongoDB, Node JS, Angular JS, Boostrap, SASS

View the project: 3derm.com

A Homepage a Day

As a follow-up to a course I took on Graphic Design, I designed a new homepage every day for two weeks, more out of interest than productivity. I tried to go for creativity over complexity. The winner of this one-man competition is the site you're looking at!

Built with: CSS / HTML only

View the project: A Homepage a Day

World Party Day

World Party Day is a fictional event celebrating everything everywhere. Many worlds, many people, one large nonexistent party. Please explore, play, so on!

Built with: Paper.js, jQuery, HTML5 etc.
